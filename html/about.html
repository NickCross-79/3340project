About Lancer Loot

A student-built marketplace for buying and selling locally.

;margin:0 auto 36px;text-align:center;color:var(--muted);line-height:1.7;"> Lancer Loot connects local buyers and sellers in one convenient place — browse listings, post items, manage your cart, and track your orders all from one site.

Browse & Buy

Explore listings across electronics, furniture, clothing, books, and more. Click any item to view full details and add it to your cart.

Sell Your Stuff

Post a listing in minutes. Set your price, upload photos, choose a category and condition, and reach local buyers instantly.

Cart & Checkout

Add items to your cart, review your order, and check out securely. Choose delivery or local pickup at your convenience.

Your Profile

Track your order history, manage your active listings, and keep your account details up to date from your personal profile page.

Why We Built This

Lancer Loot was created as a class project for COMP3340 at the University of Windsor. It demonstrates a full-stack web application built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL — designed to be responsive, easy to use, and straightforward to extend with new features.
