Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about using Lancer Loot.

Creating Listings

How Do I Create A Listing?

To create a listing, go to your user profile, which you can find on the top right of the page, and then from there, click the button to add an item that you want to sell. There, you will need to put a bunch of information about the item you want to sell such as the name of the item, the condition of the item, and the category. Once you fill out all the information, you just need to submit the item. The item will be shared on the website based on the information provided.

Can I Edit Or Delete My Listing?

Yes, you can. To do so, click on the edit button by the listing found on your profile when you are logged into your account. And to delete a listing, click the delete button that is beside your listings. A prompt will appear to confirm that you want to delete the listing just in case you accidentally clicked it.

What Does Each Field Mean?

  • Title: Name of the item.
  • Price: The cost that the seller would like to sell the item for.
  • Category: What the item relates to. Example: a chair is a piece of furniture.
  • Description: Additional information about the item.
  • Condition: The quality of the item. The quality can range from brand new to only good for parts.
  • Image: A picture of what the item looks like.
  • Quantity: The number of items being sold that are the same.
  • Location: Where the seller is located.

How Do I Upload An Image?

Once you have opened up the page to create a listing (see above), there will be an option inside the page to upload an image. Click that option and then choose the images you would like to use for the listing from your computer.

Account

How Do I Make An Account?

To make an account, click on the profile icon on the top right of any page. When you go to that page, it will give you the option to log in or create an account. Click on the “create an account” button and follow the form. You will need an email address.

How Do I Log In?

To log in, click on the profile icon on the top right of any page. If you aren’t logged in already, it will give you the option to log in or create an account. Enter the email address you used to create the account and the associated password.

How Do I Change My Password?

First you need to be logged into your account. If you don’t remember your password, there is an option to reset it on the login page.

If you are already logged in, click your profile icon, go to Settings › Security, and use the password section to set a new password. A verification email will be sent to confirm the change.

Browsing & Search

How Do I Search For An Item?

On the home page, type the name of the product you are looking for into the search bar and press Search.

How Do I Filter Search Results?

Once you have entered a term in the search bar, there will be toggles to narrow down results by location, price, and condition.

How Do I Buy An Item?

Click on the item you want to purchase to open its listing page. From there you can add it to your cart and proceed to checkout.

What Information Does A Listing Show?

  • Title: Name of the item.
  • Price: The asking price.
  • Category: The type of item.
  • Description: Additional details from the seller.
  • Condition: Quality of the item.
  • Image: Photo(s) of the item.
  • Quantity: Number of units available.
  • Location: Where the seller is located.
Cart

Adding Items

Users can add products to their cart while browsing the marketplace. Once added, the selected items appear in the cart page.

Reviewing the Cart

The cart page shows the selected products, quantities, prices, and subtotals so users can review their order before checkout.

Updating or Removing Items

Users can remove products from the cart if they want to make changes before placing an order.

Continuing to Checkout

After reviewing the cart, users can continue to the checkout page to complete their purchase.

Checkout

Entering Information

During checkout, users provide shipping details, contact information, and payment method.

Reviewing the Order

The checkout page shows an order summary so users can confirm the selected items, quantities, and total price.

Placing the Order

Once all information has been entered and reviewed, the user can submit the order to complete the purchase.

Profile

Viewing Account Details

The profile page allows users to see account information such as their name, email, and other personal details.

Updating Information

Users can edit profile details such as contact information and saved addresses.

Account Features

The profile area also includes order history and active listing management.

Contact a Seller

How To Contact A Seller?

Go to one of the seller’s listing pages and click the “Message Seller” button. A messaging page will appear where you can communicate with that person.

How Do I Send A Message?

On the messaging page, type your message into the text bar and click the ‘Send’ button.

Where To View Messages?

Go to your Profile and click the ‘Messages’ tab to see your message history with other users.

Orders & Payments

Order Placement

An order is placed after you finish reviewing the cart and completing checkout.

Payment

Payment information is entered during checkout. We support Credit Card, Debit Card, and Cash on Pickup.

Confirmation and Support

After an order is submitted you will receive confirmation. If there are any issues, use the Contact Us page for assistance.
